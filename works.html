Plastic Mausoleum will be Tegan's debut chapbook of poetry. The manuscript was a semifinalist for the Black River Chapbook Competition.

Vascular Manslaughter is a book arts project that served as a precurser to Plastic Mausoleum.

Her next work will be a multigenre book about the intergenerational trauma of immigration.
